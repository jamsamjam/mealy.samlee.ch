Frequently asked questions

How accurate are the nutrition targets?
Targets are computed with official DRI EER formulas and MyPlate.gov methodology, with adjustments for age, activity, and pregnancy/lactation.
Is my data private?
Yes. Mealy stores data locally by default. You can export JSON, and optional cloud sync is on the roadmap.
Can I edit or delete meals?
Absolutely. Use the Calendar to edit portions, notes, and attachments, or delete with confirmation.
Does Mealy work offline?
Yes. Search results and your data are cached so you can log even without a connection.
